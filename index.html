STARTED FROM THE BOTTOM, NOW WE HERE AND STILL HAVE A LONG WAY HAVE TO GO.

Sleepy Owl started brewing back in 2016, when three friends who shared the same vision came together to change the coffee game. Their vision was pretty simple, they wanted to introduce people to really good coffee and make it accessible to everyone.They started with one product - the Cold Brew Box, in one city. Today, Sleepy Owl has over 30 distinct coffees, exclusive merchandise and accessories, that are delivered all over India. Sleepy Owl coffee has become a staple in many homes today.Our first year was promising. The founders went on a recce, received the first samples and started brewing coffee in their own kitchens. They had to get the perfect ratios figured out before creating the final brew. After trials and sampling, the cold brew was invented. Sleepy Owl took part in many events at the time because each event was an opportunity to take our product to our fellow coffee lovers out there.Our first year was promising.
